What is Brawl Mystery?

Brawl Mystery is a free, daily online quiz for all Brawl Stars fans. The concept is inspired by Wordle: every day there's a secret Brawler you need to guess. But instead of a simple word game, Brawl Mystery offers 4 different game modes with completely different mechanics.

The game tests your knowledge of Brawler attributes, images, emojis and character descriptions from the official Brawl Stars universe. New challenges every day â€“ perfect for your daily Brawl Stars fix alongside the actual game.

Classic Mode

Classic Mode is the heart of Brawl Mystery and the best starting point for new players. You enter Brawler names and get detailed feedback in a comparison table for each guess.

How the comparison table works

After each guess, these attributes are compared:

  • Rarity: Starting, Rare, Super Rare, Epic, Mythic, Legendary, Ultra Legendary
  • Role: Damage Dealer, Tank, Assassin, Support, Marksman, Controller, Artillery
  • Range: Short, Medium, Long, Very Long
  • Speed: Slow, Normal, Fast, Very Fast
  • Health: Very Low, Low, Medium, High, Very High
  • Release Year: The year the Brawler was added to the game

Green cells mean: Correct! Arrows indicate whether the target Brawler has higher or lower values. Each guess brings you closer to the answer.

Pixel Mode

In Pixel Mode, you start with a heavily pixelated image of a Brawler. The image becomes slightly clearer with each wrong guess â€“ but only slightly! You have up to 8 attempts before the full image is revealed.

Tips for Pixel Mode

The most important skill in Pixel Mode is knowing the color schemes of Brawlers. Each Brawler has a distinctive color design:

  • Golden/yellow tones: Often Brawlers like Tick, Brock, or Chester
  • Blue tones: Commonly Spike, Gale, or Sandy
  • Dark/purple tones: Think Mortis, Tara, or Leon
  • Red/orange tones: El Primo, Amber, Ash

Also look for silhouettes and special features: Do they carry weapons? Special headgear? Hats, cylinders, or big eyes can help you identify them early.
